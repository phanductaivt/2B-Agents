# BA Rules

- Clarify the requirement before writing a detailed story.
- Turn key business flows into BPMN-style Mermaid when a visual process is helpful.
- Use BRD for business-facing explanation and FRS for functional handoff.
- Use feature breakdown to structure scope into Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3 features.
- Treat feature structuring as part of BA work, not as a separate agent handoff.
- Use feature grouping to show how related capabilities belong together in one product structure.
- Use scope structuring to separate the first delivery slice from later expansion when needed.
- Keep feature groups logical, non-overlapping, and easy for stakeholders to read.
- Identify missing business rules early.
- Highlight handoffs, approvals, and edge cases.
- Use AS-IS and TO-BE framing when process change is involved.
- Keep the business language consistent with the glossary.

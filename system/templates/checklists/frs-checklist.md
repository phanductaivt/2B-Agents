# FRS Checklist

- [ ] Scope is clearly stated.
- [ ] Functional requirements are listed.
- [ ] Main flow is complete and ordered.
- [ ] Alternative and exception flows are included.
- [ ] Business rules are included.
- [ ] Edge cases are included.
- [ ] Data handling is included.
- [ ] Open questions are included.
- [ ] Linked IDs (REQ/BRD/FR) are shown.
- [ ] Traceability links are present.
